Go to Students > Add Student, fill in the required information including personal details, academic records, and upload necessary documents. Click Submit to register the student.
Navigate to Students > Bulk Upload, download the template, fill it with student data, and upload the file. The system will validate and import the records automatically.
Go to Events > Create Event, select the company, specify job role, date, and requirements. Students can then register for the drive through their portal.
Go to Profile > Account Settings > Change Password. Enter your current password and set a new one. You can also contact support for password reset assistance.
Only users with Admin or Placement Officer roles can approve student registrations. Check the pending approvals in Students > Manage Students section.

About Placement Portal

Empowering students, connecting companies, and streamlining campus recruitment through innovative technology

About the Portal

The Placement Portal is a centralized platform designed to streamline campus recruitment activities. It connects students, companies, and placement coordinators, ensuring a transparent, efficient, and data-driven placement process.

For Students

Register profiles, apply to drives, track applications, and access placement opportunities

For Companies

Post job openings, schedule drives, access student profiles, and manage recruitment

For Admins

Manage users, oversee drives, generate reports, and maintain system operations

Our Vision

"To empower every student with equal placement opportunities through a transparent and smart digital platform that bridges the gap between talent and opportunity."

Our Mission

  • Simplify placement workflows for all stakeholders
  • Provide data insights for informed decision making
  • Connect students with top recruiters seamlessly
  • Ensure transparency in the recruitment process

Frequently Asked Questions

Students can register by clicking on the registration link, filling out their personal and academic details, uploading required documents, and submitting for admin approval.

Companies need to register on the portal, get approved by admin, and then they can post job drives with details like job description, eligibility criteria, and schedule.

For technical issues, please contact our portal admin at abhishek@college.edu or call +91 1234567890 during support hours.

Students can log into their dashboard to view application status, upcoming drives, and placement results in real-time.

The portal works on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with internet connection. Mobile responsive design ensures compatibility across devices.
